The Science

What we analyse

Volume & Intensity

Total training volume (sets × reps × load) and relative intensity determine whether training drives hypertrophy, strength, or power adaptations.

Training Frequency

How often you train each movement pattern directly impacts the rate and type of physiological adaptation you can achieve per week.

Exercise Selection

Whether you're doing plyometrics, Olympic lifts, or isolation work determines the neuromuscular demand and specificity of adaptation.

Adaptation Pathways

Different stimuli activate different mechanisms — from fast-twitch fibre recruitment to tendon stiffness changes and mitochondrial density.
